Frequently Asked Questions

What sports do you build sites for?
We build recruiting sites for any sport — baseball, softball, basketball, football, soccer, volleyball, track, and more. The layout adapts to whatever sport your athlete plays.
How long does it take to build?
1-2 business days from the time we receive your form submission and photos/videos. Rush delivery is available if needed.
Do I really not pay until I see it?
Correct. We build the entire site, send you a preview link, and you only pay if you love it. Payment is due within 48 hours of delivery. If it's not paid, the site comes down.
What do I need to provide?
Fill out our intake form with your athlete's info (stats, contact, coach references) and upload your photos and game film on our upload page. The more content you send, the better the site looks.
Can I update the site after it's built?
Yes! Send us updated stats, new photos, or additional game film anytime and we'll update the site for you. Updates are included at no extra charge.
What about the custom domain?
We'll purchase and set up a custom domain for your athlete (like FirstnameLastname.com). The first year of your domain and hosting is included in the $400. We handle all the technical setup.
Is there a monthly or annual fee?
Your $400 covers the full site build plus the first year of hosting and your custom domain. After year one, it's $100/year to keep the site live and the domain active. No monthly fees.

Our Story

WHY WE BUILT THIS

We're not a tech startup. We're not a recruiting agency charging thousands of dollars. We're parents. We've sat in those bleachers. We've driven the hours to the tournaments. We've watched our kids pour everything they have into their sport. Before school, after school, weekends, summers. All because they have a dream of playing at the next level.

And we've also watched the reality of what it actually takes to get recruited.

It takes money. A lot of it. Showcase after showcase at $300-$500 a pop. Travel ball seasons that run $5,000 or more. Recruiting platforms with monthly subscriptions. Training facilities, private coaches, camps, exposure tournaments. All of it stacking up while your kid is just trying to get seen by the right coach at the right time.

The truth is, the system rewards the families that can afford to put their kid everywhere. And if you can't? If you're working overtime just to cover league fees and equipment? Your kid doesn't get seen. Not because they're not talented enough. Not because they haven't earned it. But because they weren't standing in the right place, and the right place costs money most families don't have.

That's not right. And that's why Prospect Pages exists.

We built this so that every kid who has put in the work has a fighting chance at being noticed. A professional recruiting website with their name, their stats, their film, and their story, all on their own domain and ready to send to any college coach in the country. Not buried in a database behind a paywall. Not a template that looks like everyone else's. A real site that makes a coach stop scrolling and pay attention.

Other companies charge $1,000 to $5,000 for this. We charge $400 because this was never about making money off families who are already stretched thin. It was about giving their kid a shot.

Your kid has done the hard part. They've put in the reps, the early mornings, the late nights, the sacrifice. They've earned the right to be seen. We're just here to make sure they are.
